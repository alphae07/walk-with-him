# Walk With Him 🙏

> *A gamified daily companion for building a real relationship with God — not religion.*

**Live App → [alphae07.github.io/walk-with-him](https://alphae07.github.io/walk-with-him)**

---

## What This Is

Walk With Him is a Progressive Web App (PWA) built for people who want more than church attendance and memorised prayers. It's for anyone who genuinely wants to *know* God — to hear His voice, discover their purpose, live in holiness, and walk with Him through the actual chaos of daily life.

It's gamified not to make faith trivial, but because **consistency is hard** and our brains respond to streaks, rewards, and consequences. The goal isn't to get addicted to an app. The goal is to build habits that make you more aware of God in your day-to-day life — until those habits become who you are.

No pastor required. No perfect routine. Just you, God, and honesty.

---

## Features

### 📞 God Is Calling
Three times a day at random intervals, the app rings — literally — with an incoming call screen. You can answer or decline.
- **Answer** → enter a live "in-call" screen with a personal prompt and a space to respond
- **Decline** → lose XP. He'll call again
- Calls are scheduled randomly within your set waking hours
- 4 ringtone options, fully configurable in Settings

### ⚡ Rep & XP System
Every act of connection earns XP and Reps:

| Action | XP |
|---|---|
| Answer a call | +15 |
| Journal entry | +20 |
| God sighting logged | +10 |
| Bible chapter read | +10 |
| Verse of the day | +5 |
| Purpose entry | +10 |
| Mini game won | +10 |
| Fast completed | +25 |
| Book summary submitted | +30 |

**Penalties exist too.** Declining calls, skipping disciplines, and missing multiple days all cost XP. Miss enough days in a row and your level drops — with a demotion screen and a call to come back.

### 🏆 10 Levels of Growth

| Level | Name |
|---|---|
| 1 | Seeker |
| 2 | Awakened |
| 3 | Walker |
| 4 | Disciple |
| 5 | Faithful One |
| 6 | Beloved |
| 7 | Covenant Keeper |
| 8 | Friend of God |
| 9 | Son of Fire |
| 10 | Man After His Heart |

You can be promoted *and* demoted. Your level reflects where you actually are, not where you want to be.

### 🎁 Rewards — 3-Verse Messages from God
When you hit milestones (3-day streak, 7-day streak, 10 calls answered, level-ups, and more), a full-screen reward card appears with **three Bible verses handpicked for that moment** — proving God sees you, is proud of you, and is involved in your situation. Not generic. Not religious. Real.

### ✍️ Journal / Talk to God
A space to write to God with no format, no rules, no performance. Random prompts appear if you're stuck. All entries are saved privately on your device.

### 👁️ I Saw God Today
Log moments where you noticed God working in your day — peace you didn't expect, a provision, an inner warning, a door that opened. Over time you'll start recognising His fingerprints everywhere.

### 📖 Bible Reading Log
Log every chapter you read, which book, and one thing you noticed about God. Track books touched. Build a history of the Word working in you.

### 🧭 Purpose Journal
Six dedicated sections for the big questions:
- Who Am I? (identity, gifts, calling)
- NYSC Posting
- Marriage & Relationships
- Career & Business
- Where to Settle
- Holiness & Character

Each section has a targeted prayer prompt to help you tune in to what God is saying — not just what you're thinking.

### 🎮 6 Mini Games
Games that build actual spiritual knowledge, not just points:
- **Bible Quiz** — randomised questions with scripture explanations
- **Fill the Blank** — complete Bible verses from memory
- **Word Scramble** — rebuild a verse word by word
- **Who Is God?** — learn and match God's Hebrew names (Jehovah Jireh, El Shaddai, etc.)
- **Prayer Builder** — guided ACTS prayer (Adoration → Confession → Thanksgiving → Supplication)
- **Fruit Check** — honest weekly self-assessment of all 9 fruits of the Spirit

### 🌿 Grow Tab
- **Book of the Month** — a suggested Christian book with a reading prompt. Submit a summary to prove you read it and earn +30 XP
- **Suggested Sermons** — curated Nigerian preachers matched to where you are spiritually (Adeboye, Oyakhilome, Ibiyeomie, Fatoyinbo, Sam Adeyemi, Femi Lazarus, Tobi Adegboyega)
- **Spiritual Disciplines** — Fasting, Prayer Retreat, and Hour of Silence challenges with real XP rewards

### ⚙️ Settings
- Change your name
- Set calls per day (1–5)
- Set call window hours
- Choose your ringtone (preview before selecting)
- Toggle morning, midday, and evening reminders
- Enable/disable penalty warnings

### 💾 Data & Backup
All data is stored locally on your device (no account, no server, no login). To back up or move to a new phone:
- **Export** your data as a `.json` file
- **Import** it on any device

### 📤 Share
Share the app with friends, family, or a girlfriend/siblings via a WhatsApp message or by copying the link.

## How to Install on Your Phone

This is a PWA — no app store needed.

1. Open **[alphae07.github.io/walk-with-him](https://alphae07.github.io/walk-with-him)** in **Chrome** on your phone
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (top right)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. It installs like a native app — with its own icon, full screen, and offline support

> Works best on Android Chrome. On iOS Safari, use the Share button → "Add to Home Screen".

## A Note on Purpose

This app was built for one person first — a developer, founder, and believer who was tired of Christianity being a routine and wanted it to be a relationship. It was designed around real questions: *Where should I go for NYSC? Who should I marry? Is God in my business? Why do I keep falling into the same sin?*

If it helps you too, that's grace.

The whole point isn't to build a streak. It's to build a life where hearing God feels normal, obeying Him feels natural, and knowing Him feels more real than anything else.

> *"You will seek me and find me when you seek me with all your heart."*
> — Jeremiah 29:13
